Common family questions.

Is the pool safe for young children?

The pool is deep (1.5m). Perfect for strong swimmers. Not recommended for toddlers without constant supervision. We provide life jackets on request.

Do you have high chairs or cribs?

Yes—high chair and baby cot available on request, no extra charge. Mention when you enquire so we have it ready.

Can I hire a babysitter?

Yes. Lana can recommend trusted local childcare. Arrange when you arrive.

Are there other families here at the same time?

No. The villa is exclusive rental. Only your family. No shared pools, no neighbors in the villa.

Do you allow pets?

No pets. We protect guests with allergies and the garden itself. Lana can recommend local pet-sitting if you're bringing a dog.

How far from Nice Airport?

15 minutes by car. Lana arranges airport transfers (€90 one-way typical). Car rental also available on-site.
