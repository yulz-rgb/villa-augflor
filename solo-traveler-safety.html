Is the villa safe for a woman traveling alone?

Yes. Women solo travelers have booked repeatedly. Security cameras, key-locker entry, quiet location, direct host access 24/7. Lana (a woman) has hosted solo female travelers for 10+ years.

Is Cagnes-sur-Mer safe at night?

Yes. Residential area, well-lit streets, low crime. Not a late-night party destination (which is why it's safe). Restaurants and cafés close by 22:00. Nightlife is in nearby Nice or Cannes.

Can I get to the villa without a car?

Yes. Cagnes-sur-Mer train station is 15 min away. Free shuttle line 44 to Haut-de-Cagnes village. Lana can arrange airport pickup (€90 one-way typical). Not ideal without a car, but possible.

What if I feel unsafe or need help?

Message Lana immediately on WhatsApp. Emergency contact provided on arrival. Police station 5 min away. Healthcare available 10 min away. Lana has hosted solo travelers and knows how to support.

Can I use only one bedroom and avoid the others?

Absolutely. Lock the other bedrooms if you want. Master bedroom is self-contained with en-suite bathroom. You never need to use the other rooms.

What are Lana's house rules for solo guests?

No parties. Quiet hours after 22:00. Respect the property. That's it. You're a solo traveler—you'll be quiet anyway. No strict rules for independent guests.
