How Direct Booking Works

Step 1: Find & Message

Look for villas on Google, Instagram, or dedicated villa sites. Message the host directly via WhatsApp, email, or their website.

Step 2: Negotiate & Confirm

Discuss dates, rate, and any extras (chef, cleaning, transfers). Once agreed, you get an invoice with payment details.

Step 3: Pay & Receive Confirmation

Pay via bank transfer, credit card, or PayPal. The host sends check-in details (keys, gate codes, WiFi, etc.).

Step 4: Arrive & Enjoy

Check in on your date. Any issues? Message the host directly — no support ticket queue.

Why Direct Booking is Better

Lower Price

No 20–45% platform commission. Host can afford to charge less or offer more value.

Direct Host Communication

Message the owner directly. No automated replies. Faster problem-solving.

Flexibility

Negotiate cleaning, late check-in, early check-out, extras. Platforms have fixed rules.

Better for Repeat Stays

Build a relationship. Coming back next summer? Loyalty discounts often available.

No Algorithm Interference

Your message is with the owner, not buried in platform queues or seasonal pricing.

Support the Host

100% of your payment goes to the person running the villa. Fairer economics.

Safety Tips for Direct Booking

  • ✓ Verify ownership: Ask for ID, photos of recent guests, proof of ownership. Legitimate hosts don't mind.
  • ✓ Use secure payment: Bank transfer, credit card (with chargeback protection), or PayPal. Never gift cards or crypto.
  • ✓ Get a written contract: Even informal. Dates, rate, what's included, cancellation policy. Email confirmation counts.
  • ✓ Check reviews independently: Google the property name. Read Airbnb / Booking reviews even if booking direct.
  • ✓ Video call before booking: Meet the host on Zoom or WhatsApp. Scammers avoid this.
