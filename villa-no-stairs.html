Accessibility questions answered.

Are there any steps inside the villa?

Ground-floor bedrooms and bathrooms are completely step-free. There's one small step (4") from the living room to the pool terrace—manageable for most, but let us know if even this is a concern.

How wide are the doorways?

Standard European doorways (approximately 80cm). Not wheelchair-accessible width, but comfortable for people using canes or walkers.

Can I request grab bars or mobility aids?

Yes. Message Lana with your specific needs. She can arrange grab bars in bathrooms, shower chairs, or other mobility aids before your arrival.

Is the pool accessible without stairs?

One small step down from the terrace to pool level (approximately 4"). Not fully wheelchair accessible, but manageable with assistance.

Can I arrange a caregiver or companion stay?

Yes. Extra beds available in the second bedroom or third loft. Or arrange a separate caregiver accommodation nearby—Lana can help.

What if my needs exceed ground-floor layout?

Be direct with Lana about your specific accessibility needs. She's honest about what the villa can accommodate and what it can't. Better to clarify before booking.
