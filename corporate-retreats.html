Corporate retreat FAQ.

How much does a team retreat cost?

Summer rates: €520/night (6 guests). With 8 people: €65/person/night. Cleaning (€120), local tax (€28), optional private chef (€300–€500/day). Message Lana for custom quote.

Can you arrange a private chef for team meals?

Often yes. Lana has trusted partners. Subject to season and availability. €300–€500/day typical. Arrange when you book.

Is the Wi-Fi good enough for video calls?

Yes. Fibre Wi-Fi strong enough for large video conferences and screen sharing. Tested for work from home setups.

Can we have team dinners at local restaurants?

Yes. Lana makes reservations at restaurants suitable for groups. Local recommendations included in your booking info.

What about team-building activities?

Lana arranges (on request): day boat charters from Antibes, wine estate tastings, coastal walks. Available subject to season and availability.

Can we have team parties or late dinners?

Yes, within reason. Quiet hours apply (no outdoor music after 22:00). Residential neighborhood. Indoor dinners and celebrations are fine. Just keep noise reasonable after 22:00.

How do we book a corporate retreat?

Message Lana with: team size, dates, specific catering/activity requests. She sends custom quote. Pay deposit to confirm. Balance 30 days before arrival. Direct booking means direct coordination with Lana throughout.

A typical team retreat day.

Morning (08:00–12:00): Coffee, pastries, breakfast together. Work session 1. Strong Wi-Fi for calls. Open-plan keeps team together.

Lunch (12:00–14:00): Team lunch (home-cooked or reserved at restaurant). Everyone eats together. Bonding time.

Afternoon (14:00–18:00): Pool break (swimming, reading, siesta). Work session 2. Breakout groups in garden if needed.

Evening (18:00+): Sunset on terrace. Dinner (home or restaurant). Wine, conversation. Team bonding without work pressure.
