Adult guest questions.

Can we have friends visit during our stay?

Yes. You have three bedrooms. Friends can stay in the second or loft bedroom at no extra charge. Day visitors are welcome.

Are quiet hours enforced?

Yes. No outdoor music after 22:00. That's why it's quiet—because everyone respects it. Parties welcome elsewhere on the Riviera, not here.

Can we arrange a private chef?

Yes. Lana has trusted local chefs. Available on request (subject to season and availability). Sommelier-led wine tastings also arranged through partners.

Is there work space for remote work?

Yes. Fibre Wi-Fi is fast (strong enough for video calls). Open-plan living has a natural workspace. A/C throughout keeps you cool.

What's the vibe—luxury hotel or friend's villa?

Friend's villa. Designed, not over-decorated. Everything functions perfectly. Direct relationship with Lana, not a corporate chain experience.

Do you host adult parties?

No. Residential area with quiet hours. We welcome dinner parties, wine tastings, game nights—but not ragers. Not the right villa for that.
