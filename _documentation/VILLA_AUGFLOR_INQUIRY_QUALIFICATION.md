## PRE-MORTEM: Scoring Pitfalls

### ⚠️ TRAP 1: Overweighting source channel
- **Problem:** Assumption that Airbnb inquiries are pre-qualified (they're not always)
- **Fix:** Look at timeline + specificity more than source

### ⚠️ TRAP 2: Bias toward repeat guests
- **Problem:** Giving all past guests automatic high scores
- **Fix:** Score fairly — a past guest with vague dates (score 35) gets less priority than new German couple with specific dates (score 88)

### ⚠️ TRAP 3: Ignoring low-score inquiries
- **Problem:** Discounting 20-30 score inquiries
- **Fix:** They convert at 8-12% with proper nurture. Don't ignore, just deprioritize.

### ⚠️ TRAP 4: Not updating scores as inquiry evolves
- **Problem:** First inquiry scores 25 (generic). Guest replies with specific dates. Still showing as 25.
- **Fix:** Re-score on each reply. A follow-up reply often changes score by 20-30 points.
