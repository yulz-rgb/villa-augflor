## THE 3-SECOND SCORING SYSTEM

When an inquiry arrives, quickly score it:

| Factor | Points | Check |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Source:** Direct contact | 20 | Email/WhatsApp? |
| **Dates:** Specific dates given? | 25 | "July 5-12" or just "summer"? |
| **Market:** German/UK? | 20 | Where are they from? |
| **Quality:** Asked specific questions? | 15 | Did they read the listing? |
| **Group:** How many guests, how many nights? | 20 | 5+ guests, 7+ nights = full points |

**Total: 0-100**

- **80+:** 🔴 Send WhatsApp NOW (within 30 min)
- **60-79:** 🟠 Send email today
- **30-59:** 🟡 Send nurture sequence (Agent handles)
- **<30:** 🟢 Add to drip list (Agent handles)
