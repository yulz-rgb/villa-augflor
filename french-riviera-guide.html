Essential French Riviera Destinations

Nice: The Main City

Promenade des Anglais, Vieux-Nice markets, Musée Matisse. Stay 2–3 days for the main sights. Best beaches: Promenade des Anglais beach clubs.

Why Villa Augflor works: 25 min to Nice centre. Day-trip friendly with private pool to retreat to.

Antibes: Markets & Culture

Marché Provençal (unmissable at 9am). Picasso Museum. Old town ramparts. Superyacht-watching at the port.

Why Villa Augflor works: 25 min away. Perfect base for Antibes weekends.

Cannes: Beach Clubs & Festivals

Croisette beach clubs, Palais des Festivals, island ferries to Îles de Lérins. Peak crowds May and July–August.

Why Villa Augflor works: 35 min away, quieter accommodation option.

Hill Villages: Timeless France

Saint-Paul-de-Vence (galleries), Vence (Matisse Chapel), Èze (panoramic views), Grasse (perfume capital).

Why Villa Augflor works: Central base for exploring hinterland villages.

The Local Market Experience

French Riviera markets are where locals actually eat. Open daily until 1–2pm. Arrive early, bring an empty bag, buy without a plan.

Marché Provençal, Antibes

25 min from villa. Fresh produce, olives, charcuterie, socca street food. 9am–1pm daily.

Local tip: Go at 9:15am, order socca from the stand, people-watch from a café.

Cours Saleya, Nice

25 min from villa. Flowers, fruit, vegetables, seafood. Morning energy is incredible.

Local tip: Monday–Saturday for produce. Sunday flowers only.

Where Locals Eat (Not Tourists)

Skip the port-side tourist traps. Real Riviera dining happens in quiet squares, fish shacks, and hole-in-the-wall trattorias.

La Pesquière, Cros-de-Cagnes

15 min from villa. Seafront fish restaurant locals don't advertise. Order dorade grillée, Bellet white wine, stay for coffee.

Haut-de-Cagnes at Dusk

5 min from villa. Medieval lanes, Renaissance château, cold rosé at the square. Free shuttle from Square Bourdet.

Cap d'Antibes Coastal Path

25 min from villa. Sentier du Littoral: crystal water, hidden swimming spots, shoulders burning by noon. Pack a picnic.

Gorges du Loup

45 min from villa. Dramatic limestone gorges, cool in August. Combine with Gourdon village at the top for panoramic views.

When to Visit: Seasons on the Riviera

June & September (Shoulder)

Lower crowds, warm sea, calmer roads. Best value period. 70% of summer's experience at 60% of summer's price.

July & August (Peak)

Highest demand, packed beaches, highest prices. Cannes Film Festival (May). Book 3+ months ahead.

Check Villa Augflor availability for your preferred season.

Getting Around the Riviera

By Car

Hire a car for exploring villages and coastal drives. Roads are excellent. Parking in Nice/Antibes can be tight. Villa Augflor has private parking.

By Train (SNCF)

Fast coastal train: Nice–Antibes–Cannes. Not scenic, but convenient. Buy a carnet of 10 tickets for discounts.

By Bus (Lignes d'Azur)

Cheap, local buses. Line 44 from Cagnes to Haut-de-Cagnes is unmissable. Slower, but you see real Riviera.

By Taxi/Transfer

Private transfers from Nice Airport. Villa Augflor can arrange quotes. Door-to-door convenience.

Planning Your Riviera Stay

5-Day Itinerary Example

Day 1: Arrive Nice, explore Promenade des Anglais and Vieux-Nice.

Day 2: Marché Provençal (Antibes), old town ramparts, Picasso Museum.

Day 3: Pool day, hill village (Saint-Paul or Èze), sunset dinner in Haut-de-Cagnes.

Day 4: Cannes (Croisette, island ferry), beach clubs, or Cap d'Antibes coastal walk.

Day 5: Slow morning, final swim, explore local restaurants, depart.

Where to stay: Villa Augflor is central to all these destinations.
