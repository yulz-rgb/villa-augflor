## Presentation system

- **Aspect ratio:** 4:3 (`aspect-ratio: 4 / 3` on `.ag-media`)
- **Crop:** `object-fit: cover` + per-image `object-position` in `area-image-meta.js`
- **Formats:** WebP variants at 480w / 960w / 1280w (+ AVIF where generated)
- **Lazy loading:** `loading="lazy"` on all card images
- **Dimensions:** `width="800" height="600"` on `<img>` to prevent CLS
- **Captions:** `.ag-photo-caption` on cards where regional photo needs context
- **Credits:** `.ag-photo-credit` + `assets/photos/area/CREDITS.txt`
