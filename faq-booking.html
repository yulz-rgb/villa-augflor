Direct vs platforms.

Why book direct instead of Airbnb?

Direct: No platform fees (9-16% saving), instant host confirmation, direct WhatsApp access, flexibility on edge cases, transparent pricing. Airbnb: Guest fees, slower message chains, platform support queues, fixed policies, less flexibility.

Is direct booking less protected than platforms?

No. You pay by card (charge-back protected) or bank transfer (standard banking protections). Lana is a verified host with 100+ reviews and 10+ years history. Direct booking means you know who you're dealing with.

You receive a written confirmation via WhatsApp/email with: villa details, dates, nightly rate, cleaning fee, tax, security deposit, payment details, check-in instructions, Lana's direct contact. Everything documented.
