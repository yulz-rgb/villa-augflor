Sample week at a glance

Day 1 · Arrival

Nice Airport → villa (13 min). Pool, garden dinner, supermarket run at Polygone Riviera (2 min).

Day 2 · Saint-Paul-de-Vence

Hill village, galleries, lunch with a view. Home for pool time before sunset.

Day 3 · Nice

Old Town, Promenade, Matisse/Chagall museums. Train option from Cagnes station.

Day 4 · Beach day

Cros-de-Cagnes or Juan-les-Pins. Evening at the villa — outdoor dining.

Day 5 · Antibes

Old port, ramparts, Picasso museum. Cap d'Antibes walk if you have energy.

Day 6 · Cannes or Èze

La Croisette and Suquet, or Èze village and perfume factories.

Day 7 · Monaco (optional)

Half-day Monte-Carlo, return for a final pool evening. Or slow villa day.

Day 8 · Departure

Check-out 10:00. Short drive back to Nice Airport.
