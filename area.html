Getting there & around

Car-free or chauffeured — your choice.

From Nice Airport

15 minutes by car via the A8. Private airport transfer on request. Car rental at the airport is recommended if you want full freedom to explore the region.

By train (no car needed)

Cagnes-sur-Mer station runs direct to Nice (≈12 min), Antibes, Cannes (≈25 min) and Monaco (≈35 min). The scenic coastal line also serves Villefranche, Èze-sur-Mer and Menton.

Free shuttle to Haut-de-Cagnes

Line 44 (navette gratuite) from Square Bourdet, every 20 minutes, climbs straight to the medieval village — skip the parking entirely.

Concierge & chauffeured days

Wine-estate tours, yacht charters, Monaco and Saint-Tropez days can all be arranged with a private driver, so no one has to drive. Just ask.

Good to know

Area guide FAQ.

What is there to do near Villa Augflor?

The villa sits in Cagnes-sur-Mer, in the exact centre of the French Riviera. Within 10–20 minutes you can reach the medieval village of Haut-de-Cagnes, the Renoir Museum, Cros-de-Cagnes beach, Antibes and its Provençal market, the walled village of Saint-Paul-de-Vence and the Fondation Maeght. Nice, Cannes, Èze, Grasse and Monaco are all comfortable day trips. Use the interactive guide above to filter by category and sort by distance.

What are the best beaches near Cagnes-sur-Mer?

The closest swim is Cros-de-Cagnes beach (8–15 minutes by car depending on traffic and parking). For sand and clear water, head to Plage de la Garoupe on Cap d'Antibes (25 minutes) or the sheltered, family-friendly Plage de Passable on Cap-Ferrat (35 minutes). For a hidden turquoise cove, Plage Mala at Cap d'Ail (45 minutes) is the most beautiful, and Juan-les-Pins has long sandy beaches with full amenities.

What are the most beautiful villages on the French Riviera?

The standouts are Saint-Paul-de-Vence (20 minutes), Èze perched above the sea (35 minutes), foodie Mougins (28 minutes), glass-making Biot (15 minutes) and the violet village of Tourrettes-sur-Loup (32 minutes). For genuine hidden gems, try Gourdon, Coaraze and Peillon. Our own Haut-de-Cagnes, just 8 minutes away, makes a perfect first evening.

What are the best family activities near Nice?

Top family days include the Oceanographic Museum in Monaco with its shark lagoon, Aquasplash water park in Antibes (summer), Parc Phoenix in Nice, the scenic Train des Pignes, canyoning in the Gorges du Loup and the Azur Park funfair five minutes away. Note: the former Marineland animal park in Antibes closed permanently in January 2025 — the guide above lists the best alternatives.

What can you do on the French Riviera without a car?

A great deal. Cagnes-sur-Mer station has direct trains to Nice (≈12 min), Antibes, Cannes and Monaco, and the scenic coastal line links Villefranche, Èze-sur-Mer and Menton. A free shuttle (line 44) climbs to Haut-de-Cagnes, and we can arrange private transfers and chauffeured day trips so you can enjoy wine estates, Monaco and Saint-Tropez without driving.
