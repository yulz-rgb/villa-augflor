Riviera playbook

Where locals actually eat,
swim, and wake up early.

5 min · must

Haut-de-Cagnes at dusk

Take the free shuttle (line 44 from Square Bourdet). Candlelit lanes, Renaissance château, cold rosé at the square. The kind of night people talk about on the flight home.

25 min · essential

Marché Provençal, Antibes

Open daily until 1pm. Local cheese, olives, socca, charcuterie, lavender. Arrive at 9am with an empty bag and no plan. The best food market on the Riviera.

15 min · local

La Pesquière, Cros-de-Cagnes

Seafront fish restaurant, the kind of place locals don't want you to find. Order the dorade grillée, a bottle of Bellet white, and stay for coffee.

25 min · hidden

Cap d'Antibes coves

Take the Sentier du Littoral coastal path from La Garoupe. Crystal water, hidden swimming spots, shoulders burning by noon. One of the great walks on the coast.

45 min · escape

Gorges du Loup

Spectacular limestone gorges, cool even in August. Combine with Gourdon, perched at the top, for the most dramatic view in the hinterland.

45 min · half-day

Île Sainte-Honorat

Ferry from Cannes to a monastic island with vineyards and deserted rocky coastline. Pack a picnic; the monks make excellent wine.

2 min · daily

Bour Pâtisserie

94 Avenue des Alpes. Open 06:15–20:00. a local favourite for croissants within a 20-minute radius. Go early, the good pastries don't last.

varied · on request

Rosé estate visits

Château Minuty, Château Saint-Maur, and private estates not open to the public. Sommelier-led tastings available on request.

Antibes · 25 min

Day boat & yacht charter

Antibes is the superyacht capital of the world. Half-day boat hire, sailing lessons, or full-day yacht charter — available on request.

Getting there & around

Practical info.

From Nice Airport

15 minutes by car via the A8. Private airport transfer available on request (price depends on date/time). Car rental at the airport if you prefer to drive yourself (recommended for full use of the region).

By train

Cagnes-sur-Mer station is served by TGV and regional trains. Direct to Paris (6h), Nice (12 min), Monaco (35 min), Cannes (25 min).

Parking

Private on-site parking for 2 cars. No street parking required.

Free shuttle to Haut-de-Cagnes

Line 44 (navette gratuite) from Square Bourdet, every 20 minutes. Skip the parking entirely.
